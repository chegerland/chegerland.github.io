Christoph Egerland
Last updated on Sep 19, 2018
PDF

Abstract

We study the role of surface plasmon polaritons in the Casimir effect between two sheets of graphene described by the Dirac model. Considering a nonzero band gap, we calculate their dispersion relation and evaluate their contribution to the free energy of the setup. We analyze asymptotic behaviour to reveal that, contrary to similar calculations performed within the plasma model, the contribution of the surface modes becomes negligible the shorter the separation of the plates gets.
